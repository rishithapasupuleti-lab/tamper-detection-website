About Our Tamper Detection System

What is Tampering?

  • ⚡ Circuit Change/Bypass: Altering wiring or creating alternate paths to bypass measurement.
  • 🔎 Magnetic Interference: Using strong magnets to disrupt electronic sensors or readings.
  • 💻 Firmware Modification: Changing device software/code to falsify data or disable alarms.
  • 🔒 Mechanical Seal Removal: Breaking physical seals or tamper-evident enclosures.
  • 🔑 Other Unauthorized Access: Any attempt to physically or digitally compromise the instrument.

Why Tamper Detection Matters

  • 💰 Revenue Loss: Tampering can lead to theft or under-reporting, impacting business earnings.
  • 📊 Inaccurate Readings: Manipulated devices provide false data, undermining trust and decisions.
  • 🔧 Equipment Damage: Unauthorized modifications can damage sensitive components, causing costly repairs.
  • ⚠ Safety Hazards: Tampering may introduce dangerous conditions, risking property and lives.

How We Detect Tampering

  • 🔎 Magnetic Sensors: Detect presence of external magnets used to interfere with measurements.
  • ⚡ Circuit Monitoring: Identify circuit bypass, abnormal wiring, or unusual current flows.
  • 💻 Firmware Checksum Validation: Ensure integrity of device code and detect unauthorized changes.
  • 🔒 Optical/Mechanical Sensors: Monitor for broken seals or tamper-evident enclosure breaches.
  • 📷 Camera/Log Evidence: Capture visual proof and maintain audit trails for verification.
  • 🤓 Smart Analytics: Analyze usage and consumption patterns to flag anomalies.
